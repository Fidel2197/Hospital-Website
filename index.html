About Us

FortunatoCare is designed as a modern hospital website that helps patients understand care options before they arrive. The page brings appointment booking, service information, doctor profiles, and patient-first messaging into one organized experience.

The goal is to make healthcare information feel less confusing. A visitor can quickly scan services, see what each doctor specializes in, leave a short note about the reason for their appointment, and find contact details without searching through crowded pages.

The page is built with semantic HTML and organized CSS so it can be extended into a fuller hospital management system with stored appointments, authenticated patient workflows, department pages, patient resources, and administrative scheduling tools.

Patient-first intake Forms ask for the essentials plus a short reason for the visit.
Clear departments Services are grouped so visitors can choose the right next step faster.
Responsive access The same care information stays usable on desktop, tablet, and phone screens.
